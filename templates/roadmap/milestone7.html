üì¶ Understanding Order Types

Different order types give you control over how and when your trades are executed. These include:

  • Market Order: Buys/sells immediately at the best available price.
  • Limit Order: Executes only at a specific price or better.
  • Stop Order: Triggers a market or limit order once a certain price is reached.
  • OCO (One Cancels the Other): Places two orders; when one executes, the other is canceled.
  • Trailing Stop: Moves with price to lock in profit while allowing upside potential.

Mastering these can help you trade with precision and reduce slippage.
